About Me

I can always say that I fulfilled my dream by working as a radio producer and technician, covering all kinds of events, especially sports, such as CD Tenerife matches in the second division and Copa del Rey, CB Canarias games in the ACB and Basketball Champions League, or iconic races like the Bluetrail by UTMB.

Starting in 2020, after being affected by furlough for a long time due to the pandemic, I decided to change the course of my professional life and fully immerse myself in the world of web programming, motivated by a great friend and the conditions and advantages that this sector offers.

Soon after, I realized that everything I was learning in the Web Application Development program was fun, sparked my curiosity, and became increasingly passionate for me each day. Specialized in the Django framework with Python, I continue learning and growing daily, trying to develop my design and front-end skills.
